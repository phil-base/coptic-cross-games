Icons of the Faith

Quick Reference — All 36 Icon-Meaning Pairs

# Iconographic Element Theological Meaning
Colours
1 🌟 Gold Background Represents divine light and the uncreated glory of God
2 🔵 Blue Garments Represents heaven and the heavenly kingdom
3 🔴 Red Garments Represents divinity or martyrdom
4 ⬜ White Garments Represents purity and resurrection
Facial Features
5 👀 Large Eyes Spiritual sight — seeing beyond the visible world
6 🤐 Small Mouth The discipline of silence and prayer
7 👤 Frontal Gaze The saint is present with you, looking at you
19 💧 Tears on the Cheek The gift of holy compunction — weeping for love of God
20 👁 Elongated Nose The saint breathes the fragrance of heaven, not the world
Gestures
8 ✋ Blessing Hand The hand raised in the sign of the cross to bless
9 🙌 Open Palms Upward The posture of prayer and surrender to God
10 🙏 Hand Over Heart Intercession — the saint prays for you
18 ✌ Two Fingers Raised IC XC — the name of Jesus Christ in abbreviation
Objects Held by Saints
11 ✝ Cross Held in Hand Victory over death through Christ
12 📜 Scroll or Book The saint is a teacher or evangelist
13 🌴 Palm Branch The saint is a martyr — they won the victory
14 👑 A Crown The crown of life given to the faithful
15 💨 A Censer The saint serves in the liturgy — priesthood
16 📖 The Gospel Book The Word of God proclaimed
17 💡 A Halo (Nimbus) The saint dwells in God's light and glory
29 ⚔ A Sword The Word of God that cuts and defends — spiritual warfare
32 🔑 Keys Authority given to St. Peter — the keys of the kingdom
36 🏆 A Chalice The Eucharist — the Blood of Christ given for life
The Theotokos
21 👶 Christ Child on Lap The Theotokos bears the Incarnate Word to the world
22 ⭐ Three Stars on the Theotokos Virgin before, during, and after the birth of Christ
Creatures and Symbols
23 🔥 Flames or Fire The presence of the Holy Spirit or divine zeal
24 🦁 A Lion Symbol of St. Mark the Evangelist
25 🦅 An Eagle Symbol of St. John the Evangelist — soaring vision
26 🐎 An Ox Symbol of St. Luke the Evangelist — sacrifice
27 👼 A Winged Man Symbol of St. Matthew the Evangelist — Christ's humanity
28 🕊 A Dove The Holy Spirit descending — peace and purity
30 🐟 A Fish ICHTHYS — Jesus Christ, Son of God, Saviour
31 🌸 A Lily or Lotus Purity and the Resurrection — new life from the waters
33 ⚓ An Anchor Hope that holds firm — the soul's anchor in Christ
34 🕷 A Dragon Underfoot The saint has conquered evil through Christ's power
35 😷 Wounds or Stigmata Sharing in the sufferings of Christ
