Historical Note: The Nicene Creed was proclaimed at the Council of Nicaea (325 AD) and completed at the Council of Constantinople (381 AD). It was defended by St. Athanasius the Apostolic, the 20th Pope of Alexandria, who was exiled five times for refusing to compromise a single word.
