Quick Reference

On Your Turn

  1. Draw a Situation Card
  2. Choose: Broad Way (0 VP) or Narrow Way (draw Trial)
  3. Resolve Trial: roll die vs threshold
  4. Optional: draw one more Trial (push your luck)
  5. Bank your Virtue Points

Trial Results

  • Automatic Pass: Gain VP immediately
  • Threshold: Roll die ≥ number to pass
  • Fall: Lose all unbanked VP this turn
  • Lose VP: Subtract from your total
