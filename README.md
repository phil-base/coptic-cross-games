# Coptic Cross Games

**Formation Through Play** — free, print-and-play games rooted in Coptic Orthodox theology, tradition, and spirituality.

## What is this?

Coptic Cross Games is a library of 30 print-and-play card and board games designed for biblical learning, virtue formation, liturgical catechesis, and meaningful discussion — all grounded in the life of the Church.

Every game is built around a **formation goal** (the character quality or spiritual practice it cultivates) and a **learning goal** (the knowledge or skill players develop). Games are designed for families, Sunday school classes, youth groups, and Christian educators.

## Game categories

- **Scripture & Salvation History** — Timeline ordering, matching, and set collection games covering the Old and New Testaments, typology, parables, miracles, prophecy, and the Psalms
- **Saints & Church History** — Games exploring the Desert Fathers, martyrs, defenders of the faith, and the great cloud of witnesses
- **Liturgical Life** — Games about the liturgy, the Agpeya hours, feast days, fasting seasons, and the symbols of the sanctuary
- **Virtue & Daily Life** — Games about the fruits of the Spirit, repentance, humility, patience, wisdom, forgiveness, hospitality, and discernment

## How it works

Each game includes downloadable PDFs:
- **Cards** — ready to print, cut, and play
- **Rules** — setup instructions, gameplay, Scripture connections, patristic references, and discussion prompts

No purchases required. Print at home or at your church.
