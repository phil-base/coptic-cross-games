## Overview

"In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth." (Genesis 1:1)

Before theology comes wonder. Before doctrine comes gratitude. Creation Wonders helps the youngest children experience the beauty and goodness of God's creation — and learn that everything they see, from stars to fish to their own family, was made by a God who loves them.

## How It Works

Cards show either a **Day of Creation** (Day 1: Light, Day 2: Sky and Waters, etc.) or a **Creation Image** (sun, moon, fish, birds, animals, people). Children match each creation to its day.

Play as a simple memory game (face-down matching) or a sorting game (face-up, group the creations by day). Both modes work for the youngest players.

After each match, say together: "And God saw that it was good!"

## What Makes It Formation

Wonder is the beginning of worship. Children who are amazed by stars, delighted by animals, and grateful for family are already on the path to knowing God. This game does not teach theology to young children — it cultivates the soil in which theology grows.

## Components

- **Cards** (24) — 7 day cards and 17 creation image cards
- **Rules** (1 page) — Two game modes for ages 4-7

## The Seven Days

1. Light and darkness
2. Sky and waters
3. Land, seas, and plants
4. Sun, moon, and stars
5. Fish and birds
6. Animals and people
7. God rested and blessed the seventh day
