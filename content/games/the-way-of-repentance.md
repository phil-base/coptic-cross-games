## Overview

Repentance is not a single moment of remorse — it is a journey back to God. The Coptic Church teaches that this journey has stages: recognition of sin, godly sorrow, confession before the priest, receiving absolution, and bearing fruit worthy of repentance. The Way of Repentance walks players through this journey cooperatively.

## How It Works

Players journey together along a path representing the stages of repentance. At each stage, **Challenge Cards** present obstacles: denial ("It wasn't that bad"), despair ("God could never forgive me"), pride ("I don't need to confess"), and distraction ("I'll deal with it later").

Players use shared resources — Honesty, Humility, Courage, and Trust — to overcome these obstacles. They must help each other, because the road of repentance is not walked alone. The Church, the confession father, and the community of believers walk with us.

## What Makes It Formation

Repentance (metanoia) is the most emphasized theme in Coptic spiritual life. The sacrament of confession is not optional — it is a gift. This game teaches the theology behind the practice: why we confess to a priest, why godly sorrow differs from worldly sorrow, and why repentance produces joy.
