## Overview

Christ taught in parables so that those with ears to hear would understand. Parables Unveiled invites players to move beyond the surface of each story to discover its deeper spiritual meaning — as the Church Fathers have taught for two thousand years.

## How It Works

Lay all cards face-down in a grid. Each turn, flip two cards. If you match a parable to its correct spiritual meaning, keep the pair and score. If not, flip them back.

As pairs are revealed, the leader reads a brief patristic reflection on each parable, connecting it to the life of faith.

## What Makes It Formation

This game teaches children that Scripture has layers of meaning. The Prodigal Son is not just a story about a wayward child — it is about repentance, the mercy of God the Father, and the danger of the elder brother's self-righteousness.

Interpretations draw on St. John Chrysostom, St. Cyril of Alexandria, and the Coptic homiletical tradition — not modern individualistic readings.
