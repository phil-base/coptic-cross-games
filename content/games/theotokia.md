## Overview

"My soul magnifies the Lord, and my spirit has rejoiced in God my Saviour." (Luke 1:46-47)

No one is honoured in the Coptic Church more than the Virgin Mary — the Theotokos, the Mother of God. Every liturgy, every Psalmody, every hour of prayer includes her name. Theotokia is a devotional card game that invites players to encounter St. Mary through her titles, her feasts, her appearances in Scripture, and the hymns the Church sings to her. It is less a competition and more a guided meditation — a game that draws the heart toward the one who carried God in her womb.

## How It Works

Each card presents one aspect of the Theotokos: a **Title** (Ark of the Covenant, Burning Bush, Queen of All), a **Feast** (the Annunciation, the Assumption, the Apparition at Zeitoun), a **Scripture** (her presence at Cana, at the Cross, in the Upper Room), or a **Hymn** (excerpts from the Theotokia of the days of the week).

Players take turns drawing cards. For Title and Hymn cards, the player reads the card aloud and the group discusses: What does this title teach us about who Mary is? What does this hymn ask of us? For Scripture cards, the passage is read and connected to Mary's role in salvation. For Feast cards, the group shares memories or practices associated with the feast.

After each round, the group chooses which card resonated most — not competitively, but as a way of focusing the conversation.

## What Makes It Formation

The Coptic Church does not merely admire St. Mary — it asks for her intercessions daily. The Theotokia hymns sung at Psalmody are among the most beautiful expressions of Orthodox theology. This game helps players move beyond surface familiarity into genuine devotion: understanding *why* the Church calls her the Ark, *why* the burning bush is her icon, and *what it means* that she said "yes" to God.

## Components

- **Cards** (40) — 10 Title cards, 10 Feast cards, 10 Scripture cards, 10 Hymn cards
- **Rules** (2 pages) — Card types, discussion flow, and group reflection format
- **Leader Guide** (6 pages) — Theological teaching on Mariology in the Coptic tradition
- **Reflection Sheet** (1 page) — Personal devotional prompts and prayers to the Theotokos

## Titles and Types Include

- **Titles**: Theotokos, Ark of the Covenant, Burning Bush, Ladder of Jacob, Gate of Heaven, Queen of All, Ever-Virgin, Second Heaven
- **Feasts**: Annunciation, Nativity, Presentation of Christ, Flight to Egypt, Assumption, Apparition at Zeitoun
- **Scripture**: The Magnificat, Cana, the Cross, the Upper Room, Revelation 12
- **Hymns**: Theotokia of Sunday through Saturday, hymns from the Kiahk Psalmody
