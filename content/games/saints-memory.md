## Overview

The saints are not distant historical figures — they are our friends, our family in Christ, and our intercessors before God. Saints Memory introduces the youngest children to the great saints of the Coptic Church through a simple, beloved game mechanic.

## How It Works

Lay all cards face-down in a grid. On your turn, flip two cards. If you find a matching pair — a saint and their symbol — keep the pair and go again. If not, flip them back and remember where they were.

The game ends when all pairs are matched. Count your pairs to see who found the most.

## What Makes It Formation

Young children learn through repetition and visual association. By playing this game repeatedly, they build a natural familiarity with the saints — their names, their faces, their symbols. This familiarity becomes the foundation for deeper knowledge later.

More importantly, the game normalizes the communion of saints. These are people we know, people we ask to pray for us, people whose icons surround us in church.
