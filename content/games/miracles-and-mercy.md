## Overview

The miracles of Christ are not just ancient wonders. They reveal who God is — compassionate, powerful, near to the suffering, and faithful to His people. Miracles and Mercy invites the whole family to explore these signs together and discover what they mean for our lives today.

## How It Works

Draw a **Miracle Card** and read the story aloud. Each card includes the Scripture reference, a brief narrative, and two or three discussion questions suitable for different ages.

The youngest can answer "What did Jesus do?" The oldest can wrestle with "How does this miracle show us something about God's character?" Everyone participates. The conversation is the game.

## What Makes It Formation

In the Coptic tradition, miracles are not confined to the past. Saints intercede, the sacraments heal, and God's providence is experienced daily. This game frames each miracle not as a historical curiosity but as a window into the mercy of God that continues in the life of the Church.
