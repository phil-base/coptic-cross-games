## Overview

The Coptic Church is the Church of martyrs — from St. Mark the Evangelist to the twenty-one Martyrs of Libya. Courage of the Martyrs places players in the shoes of early Christians facing persecution, exploring the courage that comes not from human strength but from love of Christ.

## How It Works

Each round tells the story of a specific martyr. Players draw **Trial Cards** one at a time, each representing an escalating threat: mockery, imprisonment, torture, execution. After each card, you choose: stop and keep your witness points, or draw again for more — but risk losing everything if you draw a "Fall" card.

The push-your-luck mechanic creates genuine tension: Do I stop here, or press on? It mirrors the real choice the martyrs faced — and shows that their courage was not recklessness but deep love.

## What Makes It Formation

This game treats martyrdom with the reverence the Coptic Church gives it. The martyrs are not action heroes but saints who loved Christ more than life. The Leader Guide connects each story to the Synaxarium reading and invites reflection on what faithfulness costs in our own lives.

The game does not glorify violence. It honors witness.
