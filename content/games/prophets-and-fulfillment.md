## Overview

The prophets spoke of Christ centuries before His coming. Prophets and Fulfillment challenges players to see the connections the Church has always seen — how Isaiah, Jeremiah, Daniel, and the other prophets prepared the way for the Incarnation, the Cross, and the Resurrection.

## How It Works

Each card is either a **Prophecy Card** (with an Old Testament passage) or a **Fulfillment Card** (with its New Testament realization). Players draw cards and trade with others to collect complete prophecy-fulfillment pairs.

Complete a pair, lay it down, and read both passages aloud. The first player to collect five pairs wins — but the real reward is hearing Scripture speak across the centuries.

## What Makes It Formation

Typological reading — seeing the Old Testament through the lens of Christ — is not a modern invention. It is how the Apostles, the Church Fathers, and the Coptic lectionary have always read Scripture. Every Sunday, the Coptic readings pair Old Testament passages with their New Testament fulfillments.

This game teaches the method itself: not just the facts, but the way the Church reads.
