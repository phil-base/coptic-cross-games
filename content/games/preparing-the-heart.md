## Overview

In the Coptic Church, receiving Holy Communion is not casual. It requires preparation: fasting, confession, reconciliation with others, prayer, and a heart of repentance. Preparing the Heart teaches the "why" behind these practices through a game that mirrors the preparation itself.

## How It Works

Players collect sets of **Preparation Cards** in five categories: Fasting, Confession, Prayer, Reconciliation, and Scripture Reading. Each set has three levels (Beginning, Deepening, Ready) representing growing spiritual readiness.

Challenge cards interrupt with real-life obstacles: "You're angry at a friend — can you reconcile before communion?" or "You forgot to fast — what now?" Players must address these challenges before their preparation is complete.

The first player to complete all five preparations approaches the table — but only if they can explain why each preparation matters.

## What Makes It Formation

The game teaches that Eucharistic preparation is not a checklist but a transformation. Fasting disciplines the body. Confession heals the soul. Reconciliation restores community. Prayer opens the heart. Together, they prepare us to receive Christ Himself.

## Components

- **Preparation Cards** (40) — Five categories, three levels each, plus challenge cards
- **Rules** (2 pages) — Collection and challenge mechanics
- **Leader Guide** (4 pages) — Theology of Eucharistic preparation
- **Reflection Sheet** (1 page) — Personal preparation before next communion
