## Overview

"Everyone who hears these words of Mine, and does them, I will liken him to a wise man who built his house on the rock." (Matthew 7:24)

The Patient Builder brings this parable to life for young children. Build your house carefully, stone by stone, and it will stand. Rush or skip steps, and the storm will knock it down.

## How It Works

Each player builds a simple house by placing **Foundation Cards** in order: Rock, then Prayer, then Obedience, then Love. Each card must be placed in sequence — no skipping ahead.

**Storm Cards** are mixed into the deck. When a storm hits, any house missing its foundation stones collapses and must restart. Houses built in the right order stand firm.

The first player to complete their house wins — but only if they built it properly.

## What Makes It Formation

Young children understand building and storms. They understand that a tower of blocks falls if the bottom blocks are wrong. This game uses that intuition to teach that spiritual life has an order: we build on Christ the Rock, through prayer and obedience, and the result is a life that stands.
