## Overview

The Divine Liturgy is the heart of Coptic church life — but for children, it can feel long and mysterious. Journey Through the Liturgy makes the Liturgy comprehensible by walking players through each stage, revealing what is happening and why. After playing, children attend the Liturgy with new eyes.

## How It Works

A board represents the stages of the Liturgy of St. Basil. Players advance together through the Offering of Incense, the Liturgy of the Word, the Liturgy of the Faithful, and the Distribution of Communion.

At each stage, a **Stage Card** explains what is happening. A **Question Card** tests whether the group understands. **Response Tokens** (Reverence, Attention, Prayer, Participation) must be allocated — because each stage of the Liturgy asks something different from the faithful.

The group succeeds if they reach communion together with all four response types engaged.

## What Makes It Formation

Children who understand the Liturgy participate in it. When they know that the raising of incense represents the prayers of the saints, or that the Great Entrance echoes Christ's entry into Jerusalem, the Liturgy is no longer something to endure but something to inhabit.

## Components

- **Stage Cards** (50) — Each stage of the Liturgy with explanation
- **Board** (1 page) — Liturgy journey from beginning to communion
- **Token Sheet** (1 page) — Response tokens
- **Rules** (2 pages) — Cooperative advancement mechanics
- **Leader Guide** (6 pages) — Deep explanations of each liturgical action
