## Overview

The Exodus is not just Israel's story — it is the story of every soul journeying from slavery to freedom. In Exodus Journey, players work together to cross the wilderness, managing shared resources of faith, water, manna, and prayer while facing challenges that test their trust in God.

## How It Works

Players move together along a board representing the journey from Egypt to the Promised Land. Each turn, a **Challenge Card** presents an obstacle: the Egyptian army, thirst in the wilderness, the golden calf, serpents, or doubt.

Players must allocate shared resource tokens (Faith, Prayer, Obedience, Community) to overcome each challenge. If the group runs out of resources, they must trust in God's provision — drawing from a special "Providence" deck that mirrors the manna, quail, and water from the rock.

Everyone wins together or everyone fails together.

## What Makes It Formation

The cooperative mechanic directly models the Coptic understanding of the Christian life: we are saved as a community, not as isolated individuals. The resource-sharing forces players to sacrifice personal advantage for the good of the group — just as the Israelites had to trust God's provision daily.

The Exodus holds special significance for the Coptic Church: Egypt is home, and the story of God's deliverance resonates across generations.

## Components

- **Challenge Cards** (40) — Obstacles and events from the Exodus narrative

- **Token Sheet** (1 page) — Resource tokens in four types
- **Rules** (2 pages) — Setup and cooperative gameplay
- **Leader Guide** (4 pages) — Spiritual reflections connecting each challenge to the Christian life
