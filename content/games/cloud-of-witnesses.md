## Overview

"Therefore we also, since we are surrounded by so great a cloud of witnesses..." (Hebrews 12:1). The saints of the Coptic Church span two millennia — from St. Mark the Evangelist to the twenty-one Martyrs of Libya. Cloud of Witnesses helps players meet these saints and place them in the sweep of church history.

## How It Works

A timeline board spans from the first century to the twenty-first. Anchor saints are pre-placed: St. Mark, St. Anthony, St. Athanasius, St. Cyril the Great, and Pope Kyrillos VI.

Players draw saint cards and place them where they believe they belong on the timeline. Each card includes the saint's name, dates, a one-line summary, and a small icon. Correct placements score; incorrect placements are corrected and stay — so the timeline grows richer with every mistake.

## What Makes It Formation

The communion of saints is not a doctrine to be memorized — it is a relationship to be lived. As the timeline fills, players begin to feel the weight and beauty of two thousand years of faithfulness. The martyrs of the third century and the martyrs of the twenty-first century are part of the same story.

St. Mark the Evangelist, St. Anthony the Great, St. Paul the First Hermit, St. Athanasius, St. Cyril of Alexandria, St. Mary of Egypt, St. Moses the Black, St. Macarius the Great, St. Pachomius, St. Shenouda the Archimandrite, Pope Dioscorus, St. Samuel the Confessor, Pope Kyrillos VI, the 21 Martyrs of Libya, and forty-five more.
