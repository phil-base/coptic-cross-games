## Using Games in Your Church

Coptic Cross Games are designed to fit naturally into church life. Whether you serve in Sunday school, lead a youth group, or coordinate a formation program, our games provide structured, purposeful activities rooted in Orthodox theology and tradition.

## Sunday School

Our games are built with Coptic Sunday school in mind. Each game targets a specific age group and formation goal, making it easy for servants to integrate games into their lessons.

- [Saints Memory](/games/saints-memory/) — Matching Coptic saints to their symbols
- [Feast Day Match](/games/feast-day-match/) — Learning the feasts of the Church
- [Creation Wonders](/games/creation-wonders/) — The seven days of creation
- [Fruits of the Spirit](/games/fruits-of-the-spirit/) — Galatians 5:22-23 as a card game
- [The Patient Builder](/games/the-patient-builder/) — The parable of the wise builder

- [Symbols of the Sanctuary](/games/symbols-of-the-sanctuary/) — Church furnishings and their meanings

**For teens (ages 13-17):**

- [The Narrow Way](/games/the-narrow-way/) — Virtue and perseverance
- [Prophets and Fulfillment](/games/prophets-and-fulfillment/) — OT prophecy and NT fulfillment

## Youth Groups

Games lower barriers to participation, create shared experiences, and give young people a framework for discussing difficult topics in light of the faith.

**How to use them:**

1. Print the game materials before your session
2. Read the Leader Guide to familiarize yourself with the discussion prompts
3. Open with a brief prayer
4. Play the game (15-30 minutes)
5. Use the discussion guide to debrief (10-15 minutes)
6. Close with prayer or a reflection from the Church Fathers

## Retreats & Special Events

Games make excellent retreat activities:

- **Icebreakers** that go deeper than typical get-to-know-you activities
- **Session starters** that introduce a theme through experience before teaching
- **Evening activities** that continue the day's formation in a relaxed setting
- **Lenten preparation** — games on repentance, fasting, and the Psalms

## Seasonal Use

Many games connect naturally to the liturgical calendar:

- **Advent / Nativity Fast:** Preparing the Heart, The Agpeya Hours
- **Great Lent:** The Way of Repentance, The Narrow Way, Forgiveness First
- **Holy Week:** Courage of the Martyrs, Prophets and Fulfillment
- **Feast of Nayrouz:** Cloud of Witnesses, Courage of the Martyrs
- **Feast of the Cross:** Salvation History Timeline, Types and Shadows

## Printing & Distribution

All games are free to download and print. For church use, we recommend:

- **Cardstock printing** for durability (especially for cards)
- **Lamination** if you plan to reuse games across multiple sessions
- **Color printing** for the best experience, though games work in black and white

You may print as many copies as your church needs. We ask only that you do not redistribute the files or sell printed copies.

## Bulk Printing Tips

For churches printing multiple sets:

1. Use a color laser printer for the best results
2. Print cards on 110 lb cardstock
3. Use a paper cutter for clean, consistent cuts
4. Store in resealable bags or small boxes
5. Label each set clearly
