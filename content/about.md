## Formation Through Play

Coptic Cross Games creates print-and-play games rooted in Coptic Orthodox theology, tradition, and spirituality. Our games are tools for biblical learning, virtue formation, liturgical catechesis, and meaningful discussion — all grounded in the life of the Church.

We believe that play is a serious activity. When people play together, they practice discernment, empathy, and moral reasoning in a safe environment. When play is designed with formation in mind, it becomes a powerful tool for spiritual growth.

## Our Theological Foundation

All games are designed to be compatible with Coptic Orthodox theology and tradition. That means:

- **Scripture within Holy Tradition** — We read the Bible the way the Church Fathers read it, with attention to typology, spiritual meaning, and the unity of the Old and New Testaments.
- **Sacramental life** — The mysteries of the Church — baptism, chrismation, confession, the Eucharist — are treated with reverence as the means of grace, not as topics for casual discussion.
- **The communion of saints** — The saints are not distant historical figures but living intercessors and models of faithfulness.
- **Ascetic formation** — Fasting, prayer, repentance, and self-denial are presented as the Church teaches them: as the narrow way that leads to life.
- **Communal salvation** — We are saved together, as the Body of Christ. Our games emphasize cooperation, service, and mutual edification.

## Our Approach

Every game is built around a **formation goal** and a **learning goal**. The formation goal describes the character quality or spiritual practice the game cultivates. The learning goal describes the knowledge or skill players develop.

We design for:

- **Depth over entertainment** — Our games are enjoyable, but enjoyment is not the point. Formation is.
- **Discussion over competition** — Most games include built-in discussion prompts. The conversation around the game is as important as the game itself.
- **Accessibility over complexity** — Simple mechanics keep the focus on the content, not the rules.
- **Community over individual** — Our games work best in families, small groups, and classrooms.

## Who We Serve

- **Coptic Orthodox families** looking for meaningful alternatives to screen time
- **Sunday school servants** who need engaging, purposeful activities rooted in the Church's teaching
- **Youth group leaders** serving teens and young adults
- **Churches** seeking formation resources for all ages
- **Christian educators** building curricula around biblical literacy, virtue, and liturgical life

## Print-and-Play

All our games are free to download and print. We believe formation resources should be accessible to every family and every church, regardless of budget.

Each game includes everything you need:

- Printable cards, boards, or other components
- Clear rules and setup instructions
- A leader guide with Scripture connections, patristic references, and discussion prompts
